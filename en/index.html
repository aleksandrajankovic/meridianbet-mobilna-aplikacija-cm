INSTRUCTIONS FOR DOWNLOADING AND INSTALLING THE APP

  • Allow Installing Apps from Outside the Google Play Store: Settings > Security > Unknown Sources > Allow > OK
  • Click on 'Download the App' and then OK
  • Swipe down the notification menu from the top of the screen and click on the Meridian APK file
  • Click on the button below - Meridianbet Android
  • Select "Package Installer"
  • Click "Install"
